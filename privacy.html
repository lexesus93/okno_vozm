Resume Intel Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Overview

Resume Intel is a personal career workflow tool for managing resumes, vacancies, job applications, and career-related signals.

LinkedIn Sign In

Resume Intel may use LinkedIn Sign In to identify the user and retrieve basic profile information and email address provided by LinkedIn.

Use of Data

The data is used only to support the user's local resume, vacancy, and application tracking workflow. Resume Intel does not sell, rent, or share LinkedIn data with third parties.

Storage

Resume Intel is designed as a local tool. Any stored career data is kept in the user's own environment unless the user explicitly chooses to publish or share it.
